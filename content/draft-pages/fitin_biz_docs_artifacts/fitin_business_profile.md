## Executive Summary | Find Fitness Faster
FitIn is an ethical marketplace for fitness and wellness, combined with a business management SaaS solution designed for 'fitpreneurs' and 'wellpreneurs'. The idea was born from the founder's frustration after it took two hours to book a fitness class via a Google search, whereas booking a hotel on an app like Trivago takes only five minutes.



## Market | An Opportunity Ripe for Disruption
* **Global TAM:** The "fitness and wellness" market is valued at CAD $4.8 trillion annually.
* **Canadian TAM:** The Canadian market is valued at CAD $243 million annually.
* **Target Customers (Canada):** The initial focus is on over 800,000 "fitness nomads"—individuals who pay for fitness classes without holding a membership at any single gym.
* **Target Businesses (Canada):** The market includes over 6,000 gyms, 27,500 personal trainers, 5,000 kinesiologists, and 20,000 therapists.
* **Industry Growth:** This sector has seen historical average growth of 4.7% annually. Despite a pandemic-related decline of over 10%, Statista projects a strong rebound with 15% growth until 2026.



## Business Model & Pricing
FitIn operates on a subscription and fee-based model for individuals and businesses, supplemented by advertising revenue.

| Segment                 | Pricing Structure                               |
|:---------------------- |:---------------------------------------------- |
| **Individuals** | $30/month + 10% booking fee per transaction     |
| **Businesses** | $100/month + 10% booking fee per transaction    |
| **Advertising** | $500 - $7,500/month for front-page placement    |

*Booking fees are projected to account for 90-95% of revenue, with subscription fees making up the remaining 5-10%.*

## Traction + Projections | From Zero to $1 Billion in 10 Years

> [!note] Visual Data Summary
> The source document includes a timeline and a financial projection chart, which are summarized below.

* **Timeline (2017-2022)**
    * **2017-2018:** The company went through ideation, research, incorporation, launched its MVP, and secured its first investor.
    * **2019:** Launched a beta with 17 affiliates, 25 businesses, and 100 customers.
    * **2020:** Launched the public website, growing to 2k affiliates and 400 customers, and brought on a third investor.
    * **2021:** Executed a full launch, reached 830 customers, and was in a SEED ROUND to raise $2.5M for hiring and marketing.
    * **2022 Goals:** Planned to raise a Round A, expand into the USA, strengthen the HQ team, and build strategic partnerships.
* **Financial Projections**
    * The company projects exponential growth, aiming to surpass $1 billion in revenue by the year 2028.

## Investment Ask | Return
FitIn identifies as a social enterprise, equally committed to improving physical and mental health as it is to its revenue goals. The company plans to eventually issue a class of shares to its affiliates to create an interdependent economy and a virtuous growth cycle.

**The Seed Round Ask/Offer:**
* **Ask:** $500,000 at a $2.5 Million valuation.
* **Shares:** Common shares are being offered and are negotiable. For investors contributing over $150,000, Preferred Shares and a Board Seat are offered.
* **Returns:** Dividends are expected within 3-5 years, and buyout opportunities will be created.
* **Grant:** Raised funds will be matched by an OCI grant up to $125k.
* **Use of Funds:** 50% for marketing, 40% for hiring, and 10% for development.
* **LOI Deadline:** December 1, 2021.
