**VISION:** To create a sustainable, inclusive health and wellness community that helps gym-goers and service providers achieve their physical health, mental health and professional goals. 

**MISSION:** to offer exercisers a quick, secure and easy way to book inclusive fitness classes in a wide variety of categories, and provide business owners a fair, equitable platform to sell their services.

**MOST IMPORTANT VALUES**

**Diversity and Inclusivity**: we welcome and support fitness-doers of all age groups and backgrounds, including those from underserved communities. This includes the elderly, children, new and soon-to-be (aka preand post-natal) mothers, ethnic minorities and the LGBTQ+ community. 

**Sustainability**: We believe business owners and service providers like instructors and trainers should be treated equitably by companies that host their services. We put people over profits.

**Convenience**: time-crunched exercisers should be able to quickly and easily find many different types of affordable classes near their location. We’re very eager to work with affiliates who follow COVID safety guidelines and providing online classes as an alternative.

**Community**: our aim is to create a caring, thriving and accessible community that listens, empowers and supports its members to meet their physical, mental health, and financial needs through action. Our hope is that by creating a sustainable fitness and wellness economy, we can help create a virtuous cycle whereby more options are available to people.

**Affordability**: we believe fitness should be affordable because of its vital impact on holistic health, which concerns people of all economic backgrounds.

**Education**: we strongly support the growth of the Canadian fitness and wellness industries through professional development, further education and government advocacy. We seek to educate and encourage exercisers on preventative medicine and its advocacy. We believe it’s important for our brand to leverage its social impact values by aligning with business and government.

## Four Dimensions of Tone of Voice

Serious, Casual, Respectful yet Irreverent, Enthusiastic
