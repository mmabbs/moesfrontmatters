## **UX/UI Designer** Job Description

### **Company Overview**

FitIn is a fitness platform connecting people with activities, fitness classes and personal trainers quickly and easily.

Our goal is to be Canada's largest resource for fitness classes and personal trainers, and to be a champion for both physical and mental health.

We support fitness entrepreneurs with the tools they need to focus on training their clients, rather than having to also be a marketer, accountant, and administrator. 

FitIn is also at its heart a social enterprise: it’s designed to be completely inclusive and to support all body shapes, sizes, abilities or colours. We leverage it to educate, inspire and assist people in managing or conquering their mental health challenges through movement, exercise and fitness. To that end, not only do we have categories on the platform for all different kinds of fitness, but also for different kinds of mental health and wellness.


### **Summary**

As a UX/UI Designer at FitIn you’ll create the new flow for customers on the FitIn App, one that guides them to find what they’re looking for and need, easily and intuitively.

You may work with a team doing user research to create a customer profile.


### **Overview of the Role**
- Investigate user experience design requirements for our suite of digital assets.
- Develop and conceptualize a comprehensive UI/UX design strategy for the FitIn App (customer interface).
- Provide UX design solutions through wireframes, visual and graphic designs, flow diagrams, storyboards, site maps, and prototypes.
- Design UI elements and tools such as navigation menus, search boxes, tabs, and widgets.
- Testing UI elements such as CTAs, banners, page layouts, page designs, page flows, and target links for landing pages.
- Collaborate with the marketing team, and internal and external designers to ensure the creation and delivery of tailored experiences for the digital user.
- Provide advice and guidance on the implementation of UX research methodologies and testing activities in order to analyze and predict user behavior.
- Adhere to style standards on typography and graphic design.


### **Who We’re Looking For**

#### **Minimum Qualifications**

- A bachelor’s degree and a minimum 1 year experience UXUI design experience with a web app and/or mobile app.
- A portfolio of professional UXUI design work for both web and mobile platforms
- Working knowledge of: Sketch, InVision, Visio, Java, HTML, CSS (SCSS), iOS, Android, Design Systems and Adobe Creative Suite

  

#### **Core Competency Requirements**
- Adaptability
- Communication, collaboration and teamwork
- Problem-solving
- Design thinking
- A user-centric mindset
- Attention to detail
- Creative and analytical approach
- Understanding of interaction design principles
- Business know-how: Understanding of business metrics and the ability to translate company goals and objectives into digital experiences

  

### **Accessibility**

FitIn is committed to inclusiveness, equity and accessibility. We are dedicated to providing access for all people with disabilities and neurodiverse people. All qualified candidates are encouraged to apply.

Accommodations may include: an accessible interview location for people with mobility impairments, alternative method of communication for hearing or speech impairments, providing a detailed "what to expect" for autistic people, or modified testing for a person with a learning disability.
