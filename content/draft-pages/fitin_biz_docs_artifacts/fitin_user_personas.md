## BRITTANY BLESSED (MILLENIAL PERSONA)

### Demographics
- **AGE:** 27
- **GENDER:** Female
- **MARITAL STATUS:** Single
- **CHILDREN:** none
- **LOCATION:** Toronto
- **OCCUPATION:** Marketing
- **JOB TITLE:** Digital Marketing Manager
- **ANNUAL INCOME:** \$65k
- **LEVEL OF EDUCATION:** College program in marketing or similar

### Goals + Values
**GOALS:**
* Wants to upgrade from a 400 sqft condo to a 2-bedroom, but needs to find a partner first to afford it and start a family.
* Wants to improve her physique to post pictures on Instagram and become an influencer.
* Wants to travel a lot.
* Has a love-hate relationship with the gig economy.

**VALUES:**
* It's important to Brittany to experience everything life has to offer, embodying a "you only live once" philosophy.

### Challenges + Pain Points
**CHALLENGES:**
* She feels her schedule is a disaster due to being expected at many events each week.
* She has a craft beer blog but laments about carrying an extra 8lbs of "water weight".

**PAIN POINTS:**
* Being the last to know about a new trend drives her crazy, as she considers it part of her job to be current.
* She loves being seen at festivals and events but cannot make it to all of them.
* She is regularly troubled by existential angst.

### Sources of Information
- **BOOKS:** She keeps a couple on her shelf for appearances but mostly reads what her friends are reading to stay in the loop.
- **MAGAZINES:** She subscribes to Pressed.
- **BLOGS/WEBSITES:** She follows a couple of trendy lifestyle blogs and uses blog sites to find opinions or facts to support her own posts.

### Objections + Role in Purchase
**OBJECTIONS:**
* It takes too much time.
* She gets frustrated with poor UX/UI experiences.
* It should be a simple process she can complete on her phone during a short subway ride.

**ROLE IN THE PURCHASE PROCESS:**
* She makes all her own purchasing decisions and pays for everything herself, though she sometimes wishes someone else would make decisions for her.
* She also buys things based on what her friends are buying.
* She is looking for a partner who will buy things for her, including a personal trainer.

### Demographics
- **AGE:** 39
- **GENDER:** Female
- **MARITAL STATUS:** Married
- **CHILDREN:** 2 (aged 4 and 11)
- **LOCATION:** Newmarket
- **OCCUPATION:** Accountant
- **JOB TITLE:** Manager
- **ANNUAL INCOME:** \$90k
- **LEVEL OF EDUCATION:** Undergrad + professional certification

### Goals + Values
**GOALS:**
* To continue living the lifestyle she's accustomed to and be able to buy what she wants.
* For her kids to grow up polite, respectful, successful, and want for nothing.
* To enjoy everything life has to offer.
* To age gracefully and look 39 for at least 20 more years.

**VALUES:**
* Family comes first, and she is a self-described "helicopter mom" because she wants the best for her kids.
* She loves giving advice to her friends, even if it's unsolicited, and is fiercely loyal to them.

### Challenges + Pain Points
**CHALLENGES:**
* She is so busy trying to stay young and look good that she needs help managing her household.
* Staying in her size 6 jeans.
* Wine.

**PAIN POINTS:**
* Wants to try the trendiest new fitness classes, partly to have a place to wear her expensive workout clothes.
* Finding classes for her kids can be very difficult, a sentiment shared by her friends.

### Sources of Information
- **BOOKS:** Reads authors like Elizabeth Gilbert.
- **MAGAZINES:** Loves celebrity gossip magazines like People.
- **BLOGS/WEBSITES:** Follows many mommy sites to stay up-to-date on parenting advice, as well as cooking and recipe sites.

### Objections + Role in Purchase
**OBJECTIONS:**
* She will complain about not being able to find the information she wants, not finding it quickly enough, or not having enough options.

**ROLE IN THE PURCHASE PROCESS:**
* She handles most of the organization and purchasing for her family.

## MARGE KENSHAW (FITIN LIVE PERSONA)

### Demographics
- **AGE:** 49
- **GENDER:** Female
- **MARITAL STATUS:** Single
- **CHILDREN:** 1 (aged 26)
- **LOCATION:** Hamilton
- **OCCUPATION:** Cleaner
- **JOB TITLE:** Self-employed cleaner
- **ANNUAL INCOME:** \$36k
- **LEVEL OF EDUCATION:** High school + 1 year college

### Goals + Values
**GOALS:**
* To earn enough to pay her rent and afford small luxuries.
* She is too tired to find better work, so she wants to make her current career choice more sustainable.
* She knows she needs to stay strong for her physical job without getting injured.
* She is curious about mental health classes and what they might do for her.

**VALUES:**
* She just wants to be able to feed herself and her cat and maintain their home.
* She respects her health but is not convinced fitness will help due to past negative experiences with exercise.

### Challenges + Pain Points
**CHALLENGES:**
* Her work schedule is dependent on her clients' schedules.
* She doesn't have interest in managing her own fitness and knows she needs guidance and support.

**PAIN POINTS:**
* Her work is physical and can leave her too tired to work out.
* She does not own any workout equipment.
* She worries that she will be laughed at.
* Her internet is shared with a neighbor and can be slow at times.

### Sources of Information
- **BOOKS:** Reads a lot of romance novels from the thrift store.
- **MAGAZINES:** Can't really afford them but occasionally treats herself to US Weekly.
- **BLOGS/WEBSITES:** She is only on Facebook, where she follows some gardening groups.

### Objections + Role in Purchase
**OBJECTIONS:**
* Cost is always a factor, and she needs a flexible schedule.

**ROLE IN THE PURCHASE PROCESS:**
* She is connected with FitIn Live's Pay-it-Forward pass through her community group and signs up when the schedule works for her.
