**Progressive Disclosure** — a design pattern where you show only the essential information upfront, then reveal additional detail or complexity as the user needs it. It reduces cognitive load by not overwhelming people with everything at once — they get depth on demand. 
^pop-up-2

**Film productions ≈ start-ups** — Every film production is a single-purpose company that goes through the same business cycles as any other, except on a contracted timeline. Incorporate when development gets serious; shut down when the film is out. ^935054
