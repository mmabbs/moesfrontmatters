## Logline

In 1969, as WWII rages against Nazi werewolves, a guilt-ridden nurse and the rebellious werewolf girl she rescued from the Sandman's reverse-lycanthropy experiments must survive lawless Grim Rock to find an elusive anti-war commune — if they don't kill each other first.
